What is Antivirus Software and Why Do I Need It?

Antivirus software is a type of program designed to protect your computer, laptop, or mobile device from malicious software, also known as malware. This includes viruses, Trojans, spyware, adware, ransomware, and other types of harmful code that can compromise your personal data, slow down your device, or even render it useless. Antivirus software works by scanning your device for potential threats, identifying and isolating malicious files, and providing real-time protection against new and emerging threats. Some of the key features of antivirus software include: * Virus scanning and removal * Real-time protection * Firewall protection * Anti-phishing and anti-ransomware protection * Password management and encryption * System optimization and performance enhancement Having antivirus software installed on your device is essential in today's digital age, where cyber threats are becoming increasingly common and sophisticated. Without it, you risk exposing your personal data, financial information, and sensitive documents to hackers and cybercriminals.
