Is antivirus software necessary for Mac users?

While Macs are generally considered to be more secure than PCs, antivirus software is still necessary for Mac users to protect against malware, phishing attacks, and other online threats. Even if you're not downloading pirated software or visiting suspicious websites, antivirus software can provide an extra layer of protection and peace of mind. Look for antivirus software that's specifically designed for Mac, such as Norton Antivirus for Mac or Kaspersky Internet Security for Mac.

Can antivirus software slow down my computer?

Antivirus software can potentially slow down your computer, especially if it's running in the background and scanning for malware or updating its virus definitions. However, many modern antivirus software programs are designed to be lightweight and minimize system impact. To minimize the risk of slow performance, look for antivirus software that's optimized for your operating system and uses advanced technologies like cloud-based scanning and machine learning algorithms.
