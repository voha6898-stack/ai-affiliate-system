Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between free and paid antivirus software?

Paid antivirus software typically offers more advanced features and better protection compared to free versions. Free antivirus software often comes with limitations, such as fewer scanning options, no real-time protection, and limited customer support. Paid software provides comprehensive security, technical support, and regular updates to ensure optimal protection.

Can I use a single antivirus software for all my devices?

While some antivirus software allows you to use a single subscription for multiple devices, others may have limitations or restrictions on the number of devices you can protect. Check the software's compatibility and licensing terms before purchasing, as some may require separate subscriptions for each device, especially for mobile or gaming consoles.

How often should I update my antivirus software?

It's essential to update your antivirus software regularly, usually every few weeks or after a major update, to ensure you have the latest virus definitions and protection features. Failing to update your antivirus software can leave your devices vulnerable to new malware and security threats. Enable automatic updates or set reminders to stay protected.
