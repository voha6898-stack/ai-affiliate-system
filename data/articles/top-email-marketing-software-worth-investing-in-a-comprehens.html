To measure the success of your email marketing campaigns, track key metrics like open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and unsubscribe rates. Use built-in analytics tools or third-party software to monitor performance and adjust your strategy accordingly. Email marketing automation allows you to streamline and personalize your email campaigns, saving time and increasing efficiency. It enables you to set up automated workflows, segment your email list, and deliver targeted messages to specific audiences. With automation, you can also monitor and adjust your campaigns in real-time, leading to improved engagement and conversion rates.
