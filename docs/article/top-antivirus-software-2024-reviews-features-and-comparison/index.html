How can I remove malware from my computer?

If your computer is infected with malware, it's essential to remove it as soon as possible to prevent further damage. The first step is to disconnect your computer from the internet to prevent the malware from spreading. Then, run a full scan of your antivirus software to detect and remove the malware. If the malware is persistent, you may need to use a malware removal tool or seek professional help.
