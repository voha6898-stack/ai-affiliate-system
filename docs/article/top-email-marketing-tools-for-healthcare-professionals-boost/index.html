What is the best email marketing tool for healthcare professionals?

How do I measure the ROI of my email marketing campaigns in healthcare?

To measure the ROI of email marketing campaigns in healthcare, healthcare professionals should track key metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and the number of appointments scheduled or patients treated. Additionally, they can use tools like Google Analytics to track the revenue generated from email marketing campaigns and calculate the return on investment (ROI) by comparing the revenue generated to the cost of the campaign. This will help healthcare professionals determine the effectiveness of their email marketing efforts and make data-driven decisions.
