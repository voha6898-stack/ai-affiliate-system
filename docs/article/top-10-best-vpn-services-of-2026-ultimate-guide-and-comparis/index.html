Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a free VPN and a paid VPN?

A free VPN usually comes with limitations, such as data caps, slower speeds, and advertisements. Paid VPNs, on the other hand, offer more features, faster speeds, and better security. While free VPNs can be a good starting point, paid VPNs are generally more reliable and offer better performance.

Can I use a VPN on multiple devices at the same time?

Yes, most VPN services allow you to connect multiple devices at the same time. However, the number of simultaneous connections varies depending on the VPN provider. Some VPNs may offer 3-5 connections, while others may allow up to 10 or more. Be sure to check with your VPN provider to see how many devices you can connect at once.
