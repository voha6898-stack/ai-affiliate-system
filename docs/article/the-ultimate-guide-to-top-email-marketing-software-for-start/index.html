Measuring the success of your email marketing campaigns involves tracking key performance indicators (KPIs) such as open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, and return on investment (ROI). Use analytics and reporting tools provided by your email marketing software to track these metrics and make data-driven decisions to improve future campaigns. Regularly review and analyze your campaign results to refine your email marketing strategy and optimize your results.
