Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I update my antivirus software?

It is essential to update your antivirus software regularly, ideally on a daily or weekly basis, to ensure you have the latest virus definitions and protection against emerging threats. This will help prevent your device from becoming infected with new malware variants. You can usually set your antivirus software to automatically update in the background.

Can antivirus software protect against all types of malware?

While top antivirus software can protect against a wide range of malware types, including viruses, Trojans, and spyware, it may not be 100% effective against all types of malware. This is because new and sophisticated malware variants can evade detection by traditional antivirus software. However, reputable antivirus software often includes advanced features, such as machine learning and behavioral detection, to counter emerging threats.

What are the differences between free and paid antivirus software?

The main difference between free and paid antivirus software is the level of protection and features offered. Free antivirus software typically provides basic protection against malware, while paid antivirus software offers advanced features, such as real-time protection, firewall management, and password management, as well as priority customer support. Paid antivirus software also often includes more comprehensive protection against emerging threats and advanced malware variants.
