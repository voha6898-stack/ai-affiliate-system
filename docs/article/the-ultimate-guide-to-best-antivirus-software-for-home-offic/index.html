Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between antivirus software and internet security software?

Antivirus software specifically focuses on protecting your device from malware, viruses, and other types of cyber threats. Internet security software, on the other hand, provides a broader range of protection, including features such as firewall protection, anti-ransomware, and online banking protection. This makes internet security software a more comprehensive solution for home office security.

Can I use free antivirus software for my home office?

While free antivirus software can provide basic protection, it may not offer the same level of security as paid antivirus software. Free antivirus software often has limitations, such as limited virus definitions, fewer features, and less frequent updates. This may leave your home office vulnerable to more sophisticated cyber threats.

How often should I update my antivirus software?

It's essential to update your antivirus software regularly to ensure you have the latest virus definitions and security patches. Most antivirus software providers recommend updating your software at least once a week, and some may require it more frequently. A firewall is an essential security feature that helps block unauthorized access to your network. However, a firewall is not a substitute for antivirus software.
